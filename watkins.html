Invisible Worlds

Augmented reality story telling app for the National Trust in Alderley Edge

Alderley Edge is associated with a romantic local Legend of Alderley Edge which tells of the wizard Merlin and a band of sleeping warriors who will rise and save our country in its hour of need.

We developed an innovative geo-located audio and augmented reality tour that encourages visitors to share and view stories inspired by Alderley Edge.
Visitors could view geo-located content at the edge or immerse themselves remotely via an ancient portal that transported the user to key locations using 360 video footage to inspire their own narratives.

Mablethorpe Histories

Working with the Lincolnshire county council and historians we developed a geo-location based augmented reality app that encourages visitors to explore Mablethorpe and uncover its hidden histories through an augmented narrative and post card portals.

Vestigiascope

This application turns your mobile device into a Vestigiascope.  This experimental technology has been created to enable participants to journey through city of Lincoln and uncover the ghosts of the past. These ghosts, or what scientists call "vestigia", are the echoes of history recorded onto the fabric of the world around us

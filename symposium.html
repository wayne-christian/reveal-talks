Invisible Worlds

Invisible Worlds

Augmented reality story telling app for the National Trust in Alderley Edge

Alderley Edge is associated with a romantic local Legend of Alderley Edge which tells of the wizard Merlin and a band of sleeping warriors who will rise and save our country in its hour of need.

We developed an innovative geo-located audio and augmented reality tour that encourages visitors to share and view stories inspired by Alderley Edge.
Visitors could view geo-located content at the edge or immerse themselves remotely via an ancient portal that transported the user to key locations using 360 video footage to inspire their own narratives.

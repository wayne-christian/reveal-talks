What this is

We want to use the sessions as a way to create some new connections across the new  school which may facilitate new projects for both EIG and Scholarship and in general highlight the valuable contribution we are all making to the college. The purpose of the group is to understand what the opportunities are for EIG, share experiences and identify how else we can support each other to make the most our potential and the opportunities out there.
